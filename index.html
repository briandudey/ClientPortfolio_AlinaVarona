ABOUT ME

Thank you for visiting my portfolio and for letting me share a little bit about myself. Currently, I am the faculty coordinator for the Career Advancement Academy (CAA); a grant funded program which seeks to establish career and educational pathways for under prepared individuals and under resourced students by combining Career Technical Education, Accelerated Basic Skills, and Student Support Services. In this role, I provide leadership and grant management for all CAAs and related projects and programs across the San Mateo County Community College District: nine total.  Collectively, I have over 10 years of experience in education; seven of those years in a supervisorial role, and six years in post-secondary education.

﻿​

This E-portfolio serves as a snapshot of my current work and projects with updates for the 2016-2017 academic year. In it, you will find links to samples of my writing, grants, presentations, and images from some of the events I have created, participated in, or supported. I hope it provides you with a broad scope of my education,  qualifications, skills, and values, and that through perusing, you will see how my career demonstrates my commitment to working with culturally and economically diverse populations and the urgency with which I seek to create and refine educational and career pathways for all students.
