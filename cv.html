Skyline College

January  2013 - Present

San Bruno, CA

Faculty Coordinator/Grant Manager, Career Advancement Academy (CAA)

Managed and provided leadership for the Career Advancement Academy Grant, which establishes educational and career pathways for under-resourced and underrepresented individuals through the integration of career technical education (CTE), accelerated basic skills courses, and wraparound support. Supervised program staff; provided leadership and support to eight CAA learning communities and related bridge programs, which included over twenty-five sections and approximately twenty faculty, staff, and counselors. Essential duties and technical competencies included:

Identify, create, and sustain career and educational pathways for underrepresented and underserved populations

Facilitate alignment with industry needs to develop and improve educational and employment pathways

Convene institutions, employer partners, and district partners to identify workforce needs

Plan, monitor, and manage program budgets ($150,000-$1,450,000 grant range)

Implement, track, and report grant deliverables

Articulate program mission, structure, and outcomes to internal and external constituents

Coordinate and facilitate events: program visits, faculty communities of practice, CAA team meetings, district-wide CTE convenings, and professional development workshops

Support faculty in curriculum and program development for current, future bridge programs
